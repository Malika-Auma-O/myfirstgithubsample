Welcome to Villa Teag Children's Home

At Villa Teag Children's Home, we are dedicated to providing a loving and secure home for children in need. Our mission is to give every child the opportunity to grow up in a safe and nurturing environment, and to provide them with the support they need to thrive.

About

Meet Our Children

Our home is currently home to 30 children of all ages. Many of these children have lost one or both parents, or have been abandoned, and they have come to us in search of love, care and support. Learn more about each of our children on our Meet the Children page.

Events

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs and services to meet the unique needs of our children, including education, health care, nutrition, counseling and recreation. We work hard to ensure that our children receive the best possible care and support, so that they can grow up to be healthy, happy and productive members of society.

Become a donor or volunteer
